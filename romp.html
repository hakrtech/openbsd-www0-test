OpenBSD/romp

History and Status:

A port of OpenBSD to the IBM PC RT series of machines ("romp") is currently being organized. If you wish to help out in some way, please subscribe to the mailing list devoted to the organization of, and the eventual port itself: romp@openbsd.org. More information on the OpenBSD lists may be found on the mailing list page.
