OpenBSD Gallery

OpenBSD is in use around the world on a wide range of hardware. Not all systems choose to put up infomation about themselves, but here are some that have.

To have your site added to this list, send an email to www@openbsd.org with a subject of "OpenBSD Gallery". The mail should contain:

  • A short one line description.
  • A URL (If you have one - not required).
  • A brief description of how you use OpenBSD.
  • Whether the site is commercial, non-commercial, or personal.

Monochrome (mono.org)

A cluster of OpenBSD/sparc & OpenBSD/i386 machines at City University, London (UK), running probably the largest internet BBS in the UK. Best described as 'different', with a distinctive menu based interface that you will either love or hate, Monochrome is a unique microcosm that defies description.
