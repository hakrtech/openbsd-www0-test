OpenBSD/arm32

History and Status:

The arm32 port is not supported in OpenBSD; code may be found in NetBSD.

There is currently nobody working on an arm32 port. If someone has hardware, and is willing to bring it up to date, please contact tech@openbsd.org for starters.
