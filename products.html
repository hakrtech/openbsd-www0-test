Various commercial products for OpenBSD are available from the following companies:

  • Open Sound System
    (i386 only)
    A substantially spiffed-up commercial version of the OpenBSD audio device drivers is available from http://www.opensound.com/. These replacement drivers are compatible with the audio drivers OpenBSD ships with, but they support many additional cards and take full advantage of these cards' features.

  • RTMX
    RTMX sells a version of OpenBSD which has a full compliment of POSIX realtime features added to it.
