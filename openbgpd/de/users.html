Anwender

Wenn du gerne auf dieser Seite aufgeführt werden möchtest, sende deine Informationen an press@openbsd.org .

HINWEIS: Aus Sicherheitsgründen können Firmen darum bitten, dass wir ihre Namen oder die ihrer Kunden nicht veröffentlichen. Sie würden hier dann als "Nicht genannte Firma" auftauchen.

  • DMA computer service, Niederlande
    DMA ist ein Internet Application Provider in den Niederlanden. Shaun O'Neil schreibt:
    Our customers expect us to maintain a high degree of performance, security and control on our systems and network. At the same time we must remain cost effective. The OpenBSD bgpd allows us to do exactly that, and it forms a mission critical component of our business. The skill and dedication we've seen from it's developers is unparalleled.

  • Net Fonds, Norwegen
    Net Fonds ist ein norwegischer Internet Stockbroker. Network Mananger Arvid Grøtting schreibt:
    I first switched two of our routers from Zebra to OpenBGPD after finding a problem we suspected was in Zebra. As it turned out, the problem itself was deep inside OpenBSD, solved in the then-current kernel, but once I'd made the switch I liked what I saw and we've stuck with OpenBGPD.
    I've found some bugs, but unlike in some other projects they were resolved rather quickly in spite of being difficult to reproduce, and OpenBGPD seems quite stable now. Thanks, guys!

  • IP Plus Wireless Corp, Kanada
    IP Plus Wireless ist ein nichtdominanter Anbieter, der ein lizensiertes Mikrowellen-Backboune quer durch Süd-Alberta, Kanada, mit momentan mehr als 1000 installierten Geräten, betreibt. VP Bob Kitella schreibt: We have been using OpenBSD for all of our routing, bridging, internet servers, firewalls, VPN, etc. since we started the company. Our growth had forced us to install a second fiber connection and implement BGP. We have been running bgpd on OpenBSD since August 2004, and bgpd has been extremely stable. To date, we have not had any problems - it just works. We run bgpd on a P3 866 with 512 MB of RAM. We route over 800 GB of internet bound traffic every month. We are very impressed with the work that the OpenBSD project does, and we appreciate the quality, stability, functionality and security that the operating system provides for us, but more important, we are grateful and appreciate the hard work and dedication of the team. Thank you OpenBSD.
