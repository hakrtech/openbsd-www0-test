OpenBSD/mvme88k

History and Status:

Nivas Madhur started the initial MVME88k port for the MVME187 card, but has since moved on to another employer. The port is now maintained by Steve Murphree. There is a snapshot avaliable as of 2.4. The port supports the MVME187 Single Board Computer (SBC), or anything with a 88100 processor. Supported devices include: onboard ethernet, onboard SCSI, and onboard serial. Support for the MVME197 is planned, but we need hardware to get things going. (see the "Wanted List".)
