The OpenBSD project produces a FREE, multi-platform 4.4BSD-based UNIX-like operating system. Our efforts place emphasis on portability, standardization, correctness, security, and cryptography. OpenBSD supports binary emulation of most programs from SVR4 (Solaris), FreeBSD, Linux, BSDI, SunOS, and HPUX.

The current release of OpenBSD is 2.3 which started shipping May 19, 1998. OpenBSD is freely available from our FTP sites, and also available in an inexpensive 2-CD set. To order...
 For each release to become available on CD, project funding is still a big issue since OpenBSD has no real financial backer. Every sold CD or financial donation makes it easier for us to make new releases.
