OpenBSD/amiga

History and Status:

The Amiga port is basically the same as the NetBSD/amiga port but with some additions. The major changes are:

  • support for using ISA peripherals via a bridgecard.
  • supports GGBus+ (aka GoldenGate II) and CrossLink bridgecards
  • (None of these support ISA DMA so no PC floppies support)
  • Tested ISA devices are com, lpt, ast & if_ed
  • ISA IDE drives work.
  • ISA IDE ATAPI CDROM drives work.
  • ISA ATAPI CDROM audio works.

Snapshots are made available from time to time.

The maintainer of the amiga port is Niklas Hallqvist <niklas@openbsd.org>.
