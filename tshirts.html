Number 9: the OpenSSH shirt
Front Back

Nice dark green shirt - back has the blowfish SSHeriff burying the old insecure protocols, and the front has 'SSH - Don't tell anyone it's free!' This shirt is made out of a dark green material. If you don't know what this shirt is about, check out the OpenSSH web page.

Number 7: the Crypto Globe shirt
Front Back

The OpenBSD 'ramblo' munitions blowfish with 'Make Crypto Not Munitions' on the front. Map of the world according to Crypto Laws on the back, with all-american saying from U.S. founding father Ben Franklin. Currently shipping versions of this shirt are made out of a blue "tie dye" material, except for XXXL. Future versions will be flat Navy blue.
If you do not understand what the statement being made by this tshirt, perhaps you should read an article by Wendy McElroy.

These shirts can be ordered on the web secure order or insecure order.

Number 5: the OpenBSD/OpenBSE shirt
Front Back

Front of shirt contains the original OpenBSD "Open the Daemon" picture. The back picture resulted after one of our developers told his mother that he was working on OpenBSD -- she misunderstood and thought he meant BSE -- Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (ie. Mad Cows Disease).

This shirt is made of white material.
These shirts can be ordered on the web secure order or insecure order.

Number 4: The Blowfish shirt
Front

Front of shirt contains a picture of the Blowfish, and a vague reference to how using the blowfish cipher for the OpenBSD password hash makes our password mechanism more secure (blowfish, not babblefish). People won't be able to crack your passwords anymore.. Back of shirt contains the complete source code for our version of the Blowfish algorithm (written in Germany). For more information see our cryptography page...

This shirt is made of white material.
These shirts can be ordered on the web secure order or insecure order.
