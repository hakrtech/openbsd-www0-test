Donations

OpenSSH has no wealthy sponsors, nor a business model.

Naturally, the OpenSSH project requires funds to operate, due to electrial costs, Internet line costs and the same hardware upgrades that everyone must experience. For this reason, the project sells CDROMs and T-shirts and posters. Thus, when you buy an OpenBSD CD, whether at a conference, from any sales site, or from our CDROM ordering page, you are helping to increase the chance that OpenSSH will continue to make future releases.

It is also possible to donate funds or hardware, in which case your name ends up on our Donations page.

There are four main ways fund the project with financial donations:
  • Cheques
    Simply send a donation cheque in CDN/US/EUR funds made out to Theo de Raadt (since cheques made out to "OpenSSH" cannot be cashed). (Or mail Theo de Raadt for other ideas).

    Theo de Raadt
    OpenSSH
    812 23rd Ave SE
    Calgary, Alberta, Canada T2G 1N8

  • Credit Card
    Our online ordering system can do secure credit card transactions for donations from major credit cards.
    Or you can combine your donation with an order of our other items.

  • IBAN + BIC
    For International bank transfer, people can use IBAN or SWIFT/BIC direct bank transfers.

  • PayPal
    We can also accept donations via PayPal

While donations are not US tax deductible as charitable contribution, they may be deductible as an operating expense, and if someone is so inclined, they should ask for the advice of their accountant. The same applies to other countries, where corporations can make deductible donations under the terms of "Good Will".

We have a list of hardware that we would like to see donated to the project. We would appreciate it if people would look through the list and compare it to personal or corporate junk piles. Amongst other reasons, the list exists so that project members can own devices that should be supported, so that future releases ship with better device driver support.

It is now possible to donate money to the project online via credit card or PayPal.
