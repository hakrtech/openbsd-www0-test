Mailing lists

Announcement list

The Linux/Unix port of OpenSSH has a read-only mailing list openssh-unix-announce@mindrot.org. This list carries announcements of updated versions and related software.

To subscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "SUBSCRIBE" to openssh-unix-announce-request@mindrot.org. You will be required to send a confirmation email before your request is processed.

To unsubscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "UNSUBSCRIBE" to openssh-unix-announce-request@mindrot.org. Again, you will need to send a confirmation email before your are removed from the list.

Developers list

This openssh-unix-dev@mindrot.org list is intended for discussion between developers who wish to improve upon the port and/or extend it to other platforms. The list is not intended for general OpenSSH discussion, but is a good place to report and discuss bugs.

This list is open to non-subscribers. HTML mail is NOT PERMITTED on this list. Small attachments (such as diff files) are acceptable within the bounds of common sense. The list is archived by MARC.

To subscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "SUBSCRIBE" to openssh-unix-dev-request@mindrot.org. You will be required to send a confirmation email before your request is processed.

To unsubscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "UNSUBSCRIBE" to openssh-unix-dev-request@mindrot.org. Again, you will need to send a confirmation email before your are removed from the list.

General SSH user discussion list

You can get general support (especially regarding interoperability issues) from the SSH mailing list ssh@clinet.fi. Alternatively, you can post to the Usenet newsgroup comp.security.ssh.

To subscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "SUBSCRIBE SSH" to majordomo@clinet.fi.

To unsubscribe to the list, send a message containing the body "UNSUBSCRIBE SSH" to majordomo@clinet.fi.

The list is archived by MARC.
