Project Goals

Our goal is simple: Operating systems should ship with ssh included.

In under a year, the RSA patent in the USA will expire. In the meantime, of course, people outside the USA are already allowed to use RSA patented code in their software. So we have a very large testing ground for getting this wonderful public key cryptography fully integrated into our operating system.

By the time the patent expires, OpenBSD will be ready to provide people with tools that use that technology, throughout the operating system. OpenSSH is just a small part of that; for more information see the OpenBSD cryptography page.
