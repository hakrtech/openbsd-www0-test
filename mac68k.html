OpenBSD/mac68k

History and Status:

The mac68k port has no notable architecture-specific changes from the NetBSD/mac68k port. This does not mean that "OpenBSD/mac68k == NetBSD/mac68k" as there are a number of changes in the architecture-independent portions of the system.
