OpenBSD/mac68k

History and Status:

The mac68k port of OpenBSD runs on the same machines as the NetBSD port, but it is quite different in several respects. Only a small percentage of the full system lies within the machine-dependent section.

There is a list of changes in the machine-independent portions of the system. Check it out.

For more information, contact Gene or Allen.
