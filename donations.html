OpenBSD has no major sponsors -- the project's infancy was funded by a large cash donation from Theo de Raadt. Those funds have since dried up, and currently the project is weakly funded by CD sales. A few people have made additional donations to try to bolster the project, but other donations are urgently needed. For more information...
