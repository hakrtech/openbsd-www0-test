OpenBSD/alpha

History and Status:

The alpha port has no architecture specific changes from the NetBSD/alpha port. However, it's verified and running stably. Currently Niklas Hallqvist <niklas@openbsd.org> is maintaining the port.

Projects (in no particular order):

  • Add ISA DMA support (needed for floppies & wss).
  • Add floppy support.
  • Add WSS audio support.
  • DDB support.

Thomas Graichen <graichen@openbsd.org> creates OpenBSD/alpha snapshots from time to time. You may find them on the usual OpenBSD ftp servers.
