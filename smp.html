OpenBSD SMP

History and Status:

SMP (Symmetric MultiProcessor) support is not yet in OpenBSD, but there exists a project, started in February 2000, to bring said support to at least the i386 and sparc platforms.

Resources:

We have created a src/sys branch in CVS, called SMP where the development is happening. In order to check it out, do: 

$ cvs checkout -rSMP src/sys

There also exists a mailinglist, smp@openbsd.org, for the purpose of discussing the SMP support. Look here for a description on how to subscribe to our mailinglists.
