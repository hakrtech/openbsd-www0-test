OpenBSD Frequently Asked Questions - Ver. 2.4

This FAQ is specifically designed for the 2.4 release of OpenBSD, but in some cases will work on earlier versions. This FAQ will take you thru most critical steps to setting up your own OpenBSD system. And questions will range from newbie to advanced users. Hopefully you will find this FAQ useful.

Any questions can be directed to: faq@openbsd.org
