OpenBSD Newuser FAQ

History and Status:

The FAQ started a week after the 2.1 release by Jonathan Katz. Many contributions have been made by both the OpenBSD user and development communities. The FAQ gets updated around once a month, and was transcribed from the original ASCII version into HTML and SGML by Volker Schmidt.
